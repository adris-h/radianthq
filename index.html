VALORANT

Valorant is a fast-paced, team-based tactical shooter developed by Riot Games. Combining precision gunplay with unique character abilities, it challenges players to strategize and adapt in intense 5v5 matches. Set in a near-future world, players choose from a diverse roster of Agents, each with distinct powers, to secure objectives or eliminate the opposition. With its competitive gameplay, dynamic maps, and emphasis on teamwork, Valorant has become a global phenomenon in the esports scene. Whether you’re planting the Spike or defusing it, every moment is packed with adrenaline and strategy.

ASTRA

Ghanaian Agent Astra harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With full command of her astral form and a talent for deep strategic foresight, she’s always eons ahead of her enemy’s next move.

GRAVITY WELL (C)
Activates a Star to form a Gravity Well. Players in the area are pulled toward the center before it explodes, making all players still trapped inside “fragile.”

NOVA PULSE (Q)
Activate a Star to detonate a Nova Pulse. The Nova Pulse charges briefly then strikes, concussing all players in its area.

NEBULA (E)
Activates a Star to transform it into a Nebula (smoke).

ASTRAL FORM / COSMIC DIVIDE (X)
Activate to enter Astral Form where you can place Stars. Stars can be reactivated later, transforming them into Nova Pulses, Nebulas, or Gravity Wells.

BREACH

Breach is an Initiator with explosive abilities that can stun, blind, and disorient enemies. He excels at breaking through defensive setups and creating openings for his team.

flashpoint (C)
EQUIP a blinding charge. FIRE the charge to set a fast-acting burst through the wall. The charge detonates to blind all players looking at it.

aftershock (q)
EQUIP a fusion charge. FIRE the charge to set a slow-acting burst through the wall. The burst does heavy damage to anyone caught in its area.

fault line (e)
EQUIP a seismic blast. HOLD FIRE to increase the distance. RELEASE to set off the quake, dazing all players in its zone and in a line up to the zone.

rolling thunder (x)
EQUIP a Seismic Charge. FIRE to send a cascading quake through all terrain in a large cone. The quake dazes and knocks up anyone caught in it.

BRIMSTONE

Joining from the U.S.A., Brimstone's orbital arsenal ensures his squad always has the advantage. His ability to deliver utility precisely and from a distance make him an unmatched boots-on-the-ground commander.

INCEDIARY (C)
An incindiery grenade launcher that fires off a grenade that detonates when it hits the floor, creating a small fire zone that damages players.

STIM BEACON (Q)
EQUIP a stim beacon. FIRE to toss the stim beacon in front of Brimstone. Upon landing, the stim beacon will create a field that grants players RapidFire.

SKY SMOKE (E)
A tactical ability that allows you to select up to three points on the map to drop a long-lasting smoke cloud that obscures vision.

ORBITAL STRIKE (X)
An ultimate ability that allows you to select an area on the map to launch a lingering orbital strike laser that deals high amounts of damage over time.

CHAMBER

Chamber is a suave Sentinel from France who uses his high-tech gadgets and precision skills to control the battlefield.

trademark (C)
Trap that scans for enemies. When a visible enemy comes in range, the trap will destabilize the terrain around them after a timer, slowing the enemy down.

Headhunter (q)
Activate to equip a heavy pistol. Alt fire with the pistol equipped to aim down sights.

Rendezvous (e)
Place two teleport anchors. When in range of an anchor, reactivate to teleport to the other anchor. Anchors can be picked up and redeployed.

tour de Force (x)
Summons a powerful, custom sniper rifle that will one shot any enemy it hits. Killing an enemy creates a lingering field that slows players caught inside of it.

CLOVE

Clove is a unique agent with the ability to harness nature's forces. Her abilities can heal, protect, and control areas by manipulating plant-based powers to support and defend her team.

pick me up (C)
INSTANTLY absorb the life force of a fallen enemy that Clove damaged or killed, gaining haste and temporary health.

meddle (q)
EQUIP a fragment of immortality essence. FIRE to throw the fragment, which erupts after a short delay and temporarily decays all targets caught inside.

ruse (E)
Place Clove's clouds at any location on the battlefield to settle. After launching, the clouds block vision in their chosen areas. Can be used after death.

not dead yet (X)
After dying, ACTIVATE to resurrect. Once resurrected, Clove must earn a kill or a damaging assist within a set time or they will die.

CYPHER

The Moroccan information broker, Cypher is a one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy’s every move. No secret is safe. No maneuver goes unseen. Cypher is always watching.

Trapwire (Q)
Cypher's spycam is a camera that can be controlled. While the player is in control, they can fire a marking dot at an enemy that will reveal their location.

Cyber cage (c)
INSTANTLY toss the cyber cage in front of Cypher. Activate to create a zone that blocks vision and slows enemies who pass through it.

Spycam (E)
Cypher's spycam is a camera that can be controlled. While the player is in control, they can fire a marking dot at an enemy that will reveal their location.

neural theft (X)
INSTANTLY use on a dead enemy player in your crosshairs to reveal the location of all living enemy players.

DEADLOCK

Deadlock is a sentinel-style agent with the ability to secure areas with traps and disable enemy actions. She's adept at creating zones of control to disrupt enemy movements.

gravnet (C)
Throw or lob the grenade. The GravNet detonates upon landing, forcing any enemies caught within to crouch and move slowly.

sonic sensor (q)
The sensor monitors an area for enemies making sound. It concusses that area if footsteps, weapons fire, or significant noise are detected.

barrier mesh (e)
When fired, Deadlock's Barrier Mesh disc is thrown forward, generating barriers from the origin point that block character movement upon landing.

Annihilation (x)
Pulse of destructible nanowires that captures the first enemy contacted, pulling them along a nanowire path. Cocooned enemy dies if they reach the end.

FADE

Turkish bounty hunter Fade unleashes the power of raw nightmare to seize enemy secrets. Attuned with terror itself, she hunts down targets and reveals their deepest fears - before crushing them in the dark.

PROWLER (C)
Sends a Prowler out in a straight line, locking onto enemies or trails in their frontal vision and nearsighting them if it reaches them. Hold to steer.

Seize (Q)
Throw an orb that plummets to the ground after a set time, where it will then explode and trap enemies in its radius. Re-use to drop the projectile early in-flight.

Haunt (e)
Throw an orb that plummets to the ground after a set time, where it will then turn into a nightmarish entity that can reveal player locations.

Nightfall (x)
Fire to send out a wave of nightmare energy that traverses through walls. The energy creates a trail to the opponent while deafening and decaying them.

GEKKO

Gekko is a versatile agent who combines his mechanical creations and elemental powers to support his team. He uses his gadgets to scout, trap, and attack.

mosh pit (C)
FIRE to throw Mosh like a grenade; ALT FIRE to throw underhand. Upon landing Mosh duplicates across a large area then after a short delay explodes.

wingman (q)
Unleashes concussive blast toward the first enemy seen. ALT FIRE when targetting a Spike site to have Wingman defuse or plant the Spike.

dizzy (e)
FIRE to send Dizzy soaring through the air. Dizzy charges then unleashes plasma blasts at enemies in line of sight. Enemies hit by her plasma are blinded.

thrash (x)
FIRE to link with Thrash's mind and steer her through enemy territory. ACTIVATE to lunge forward and explode, detaining any enemies in a small radius.

HARBOR

Harbor is a Controller agent who manipulates water to create barriers, block sight, and control areas. He can provide protection and cover for his team with his liquid powers.

cascade (C)
EQUIP a wave of water. FIRE to send the wave rolling forward and through walls. RE-USE to stop the wave. Players hit are slowed.

cove (q)
EQUIP a sphere of shielding water. FIRE to throw. ALT FIRE to underhand throw. Upon impacting the ground, spawn a water shield that block bullets.

high tide (e)
FIRE a wall of water in the direction of your crosshair that passes through the world. Players hit are slowed.

reckoning (x)
FIRE to summon a geyser pool on the ground. Enemy players in the area are targeted by successive geyser strikes. Players caught within a strike are concussed.

JETT

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

cloudburst (C)
Throws a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact. Hold the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair.

Updraft (Q)
NSTANTLY propel Jett high into the air.

Tailwind (E)
INSTANTLY propel Jett in the direction she is moving. If Jett is standing still, she will propel forward.

Blade Storm (X)
Blade Storm gives Jett a set of accurate throwing knives that can be thrown either one at a time, or all at once — recharges as you get kills.

ISO

Iso is a versatile agent who can adapt to many situations with his unique ability to change his physical form to suit his environment. He can solidify, expand, or contract his body to deal with any challenge.

Contingency (C)
EQUIP to assemble prismatic energy. FIRE to push an indestructible wall of energy forward that blocks bullets.

undercut (q)
EQUIP a molecular bolt. Fire to throw it forward, applying a brief FRAGILE to all players it touches. The bolt can pass through solid objects, including walls.

double tap (e)
Focus timer that enters player into flow state where downed enemies killed/damaged leave an energy orb, granting a shield that absorbs damage from a source.

kill contract (x)
Interdimensional arena. Firing hurls a band of energy through the battlefield, pulling you and the first enemy hit into the arena. You and the enemy duel until death.

KAY/O

KAY/O is an Initiator with the ability to suppress enemy abilities and control the battlefield with his grenades and suppression skills.

frag/ment(C)
Equips a throwable, explosive fragment that sticks to the floor and explodes multiple times, dealing near lethal damage at the center with each explosion.

flash/drive (q)
EQUIP a flash grenade. FIRE to throw. The flash grenade explodes after a short fuse, blinding anyone in line of sight.

zero/point (e)
EQUIP a suppression blade. FIRE to throw. The blade sticks to the first surface it hits, winds up, and suppresses anyone in the radius of the explosion.

null/cmd (x)
Overloads KAY/O with polzarized radianite energy that empowers him to emit large energy pulses from his location that briefly suppress enemies.

KILLJOY

The genius of Germany, Killjoy effortlessly secures key battlefield positions with her arsenal of inventions. If their damage doesn't take her enemies out, the debuff her robots provide will make short work of them.

Nanoswarm (Q)
Locks on to an enemy when one enters its AOE, hunts them down, then explodes when it reaches its target, applying Vulnerable — can be redeployed.

Alarmbot (C)
FIRE to throw the grenade. Upon landing, the Nanoswarm goes covert. ACTIVATE the Nanoswarm to deploy a damaging swarm of nanobots.

Turret (E)
EQUIP a Turret. FIRE to deploy a turret that fires at enemies in a 180 degree cone. HOLD EQUIP to recall the deployed turret.

Lockdown (C)
Places down a trap that detains all enemies for eight seconds. Slows enemies who are caught within the ability.

NEON

Filipino Agent, Neon, surges forward at shocking speeds, discharging bursts of bioelectric radiance as fast as her body generates it. She races ahead to catch enemies off guard, then strikes them down quicker than lightning.

FAST LANE (C)
Fires two energy lines that extend a short distance or until collision with a wall that block vision and damage enemies passing through.

RELAY BOLT (Q)
Throws an energy bolt that bounces once and electrifies the ground with a concussive blast on each hit

high gear (e)
Instantly channels Neon's higher power for increased speed. When charged, ALT FIRE triggers an electric slide. Slide charge resets every two kills.

Overdrive (Q)
Unleashes Neon's full power and speed for a short duration. Fire to channel the power into a deadly lightning beam with high movement accuracy.

OMEN

A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foe scrambles to learn where he might strike next.

shrouded step (c)
EQUIP a shrouded step ability and see its range indicator. FIRE to begin a brief channel, then teleport to the marked location.

paranoia (q)
EQUIP a blinding orb. FIRE to throw it forward, briefly reducing the vision range and deafening all players it touches — can pass straight through walls.

dark cover (e)
Fires down an orb that obscures vision; can be deployed by entering a phased world or through standard means.

from the shadows (x)
Pulls up a tactical map for the player to select where to teleport. Can be interrupted by the enemy team, or canceled by Omen mid-teleport.

PHOENIX

Hailing from the U.K., Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush into a fight on his own terms.

Blaze (C)
Creates a bendable flame wall that obscures vision and damages both attackers and defenders when they walk through it.

Hot Hands (E)
EQUIP a fireball. FIRE to throw a fireball that explodes after a set amount of time or upon hitting the ground, creating a lingering fire zone that damages enemies.

Curveball (Q)
Curveballs are essentially controllable flash grenades that can curve either left or right — blinds both enemies and teammates.

Run It Back (X)
Places a marker at Pheonix's location that he can teleport back to either when he dies or allows the timer to expire.

RAZE

Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and big guns. With her blunt-force-trauma playstyle, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a generous dose of “boom.”

Boom bot (Q)
Raze's Boom Bot will travel in a straight line on the ground, bouncing off of walls until it explodes or locks onto an enemy and deals heavy damage.

Blast Pack (C)
INSTANTLY throw a Blast Pack that will stick to surfaces. RE-USE the ability after deployment to detonate, damaging and moving anything hit.

Paint Shells (C)
EQUIP a cluster grenade. FIRE to throw the grenade, which does damage and creates sub-munitions, each doing damage to anyone in their range.

Showstopper (X)
EQUIP a rocket launcher. FIRE shoots a rocket that does massive area damage on contact with anything.

REYNA

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability is only limited by her raw skill, making her highly dependent on performance.

Leer (Q)
EQUIP an ethereal, destructible eye. ACTIVATE to cast the eye a short distance forward. The eye will Nearsight all enemies who look at it.

Devour (C)
Enemies drop Soul Orbs that last for three seconds and can be consumed with Devour, rapidly healing Reyna — overhealed health decays over time.

Dismiss (E)
INSTANTLY consume a nearby Soul Orb, becoming intangible for a short duration. If EMPRESS is active, also become invisible.

Empress (X)
INSTANTLY enter a frenzy, increasing firing, equip and reload speed dramatically. Gain infinite charges of Soul Harvest abilities. Kills renew the duration.

SAGE

The stronghold of China, Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Able to revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, she provides a calm center to a hellish fight.

SLOW ORB (C)
EQUIP a slowing orb. FIRE to throw a slowing orb forward that detonates upon landing, creating a lingering field that slows players caught inside of it.

BARRIER ORB (Q)
EQUIP a barrier orb. FIRE places a wall that fortifies after a few seconds. ALT FIRE rotates the targeter

HEALING ORB (E)
FIRE with your crosshairs over a damaged ally to activate a heal-over-time on them. ALT FIRE while Sage is damaged to activate a self heal-over-time.

RESSURECTION (X)
FIRE with your crosshairs placed over a dead ally to begin resurrecting them. After a brief channel, the ally will be brought back to life with full health.

SKYE

Hailing from Australia, Skye and her band of beasts trail-blaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Skye's side.

Trailblazer (C)
FIRE to send out and take control of the predator. While in control, FIRE to leap forward, exploding in a concussive blast and damaging directly hit enemies.

Regrowth (Q)
Skye's healing trinket can be channeled to heal allies in range and line of sight. It can be reused until her healing pool is depleted — Skye cannot heal herself.

Guiding Light (E)
Skye's hawk trinket can be sent forward or in the direction of your crosshair. RE-USE the ability while the hark is in flight to transform it into a flash.

Seekers (X)
EQUIP a Seeker trinket. FIRE to send out three Seekers to track down the three closest enemies. If a Seeker reaches its target, it nearsights them.

SOVA

Born from the eternal winter of Russia's tundra, Sova tracks, finds, and eliminates enemies with ruthless efficiency and precision. His custom bow and incredible scouting abilities ensure that even if you run, you cannot hide.

Owl Drone (C)
Sova's owl drone is a reconassaince device that can be controlled by the player. The drone shoots marking dots that will reveal the location of enemies.

Shock Dart (Q)
Sova's shock bolt is an explosive projectile that can bounce off up to two walls and detonates upon collision dealing heavy damage to players nearby.

Recon Bolt (E)
Recon bolts bounce off up to two walls and detonate on collision revealing the location of nearby enemies caught within its line of sight — can be destroyed.

Hunter's Fury (X)
Fires three long-range, wall-piercing energy blasts that deal heavy damage to any enemy caught in its line in addition to revealing their locations.

VIPER

The American chemist, Viper deploys an array of poisonous chemical devices to control the battlefield and choke the enemy's vision. If the toxins don't kill her prey, her mind games surely will.

snake bite (c)
Chemical launcher that fires a canister that shatters upon hitting the floor, creating a lingering chemical zone that damages and applies Vulnerable.

poison cloud (q)
Throws a gas emitter that remains throughout the round. RE-USE the ability to create a toxic gas cloud at the cost of fuel — is redeployable & reusable.

toxic screen (e)
Deploys a line of gas emitters that penetrate terrain. RE-USE the ability to create a tall wall of toxic gas at the cost of fuel — can be reused more than once.

viper's pit (x)
FIRE to spray a chemical cloud in all directions around Viper, creating a large cloud that reduces the vision range and maximum health of players inside.

VYSE

Vyse is a mysterious agent, known for her ability to manipulate the battlefield with her gravity-controlling abilities. She can push, pull, and alter the trajectory of objects and enemies alike.

razorvine (C)
Invisible nest of liquid metal that, when activated, sprawls out into a large razorvine nest, slowing and damaging all players who move through it.

Shear (q)
Hidden wall trap that causes an indestructible wall to emerge from the ground behind an enemy that crosses. Lasts for a short time before dissipating.

arc rose (e)
Stealthed trap that can be planted on surfaces. When activated, all enemies in its line of sight are blinded. Can be picked up and redeployed before use.

steel garden (x)
Bramble of liquid metal that, when fired, erupts from Vyse as a torrent of metal thorns, jamming enemy primary weapons after briefly winding up.

YORU

Japanese native Yoru rips holes straight through reality to infiltrate enemy lines unseen. Using deception and aggression in equal measure, he gets the drop on each target before they know where to look.

fakeout (C)
EQUIP an echo that mimics footstepsforward. ALT FIRE to place an echo in place. USE the inactive echo to send it forward.

blindside (q)
Rips an unstable dimensional fragment from reality. Throwing the fragment activates a flash that winds up once it collides with a hard surface in the world.

Gatecrash (E)

dimensional drift (x)
EQUIP a mask that can see between dimensions. FIRE to drift into Yoru's dimension, unable to be affected or seen by enemies from the outside.

MAP GALLERY

ASCENT

An open playground for small wars of position and attrition divide two sites on Ascent. Each site can be fortified by irreversible bomb doors; once they’re down, you’ll have to destroy them or find another way. Yield as little territory as possible.
